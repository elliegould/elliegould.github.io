Shake It on Cam FM

Who even are we?

Hey, great question! Sounds like you might want to be one of our intellectual listeners, many of whom actually exist.

Han and Ellie are the deadpan impetus behind Shake It, a radio show at 5pm on Thursdays on Cam FM 97.2, who struggle to talk about themselves in the third person.

We like to play hot new tracks and dress up as confectionery items. If you like these things, or indeed other things, it's the show for you!
